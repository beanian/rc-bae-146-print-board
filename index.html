The reference photo shows the classic airberlin red / white scheme: a white airframe with a fully red vertical fin and a red sweep wrapping the rear fuselage / tailcone. Click any card's colour swatch to cycle white → red → two-tone and tune it to your own plan — choices are saved.

Print in RED LW-PLA

  • Vertical fin (whole Rudder section) — R Main 1/2, R Top top front/back, R Top down front/back, Rudder 1/2. The fin is solid red top-to-bottom (white logo applied as a decal).
  • Rear fuselage / tailcone — Fuse 9, Fuse 9 Corner, Fuse 10, Fuse 10 Corner, Fuse 11, where the red sweeps up to meet the fin. Verify the exact segment split against the manual / photo — the numbering may shift the boundary by a section.

Two-tone — white base + red (mark amber)

  • Sweep transition — Fuse 8 sits on the diagonal where white meets red. A single-colour print can't cleanly capture the slash, so plan a colour-change at a set Z height, masking + paint, or a vinyl decal.
  • Engine nacelle exhaust lips (optional) — if you want the small red accent at the rear of each gondola, treat EDF 2 L/R as two-tone. Left white by default.

Stays WHITE

  • Nose & forward/mid fuselage, wings & flaps/ailerons, horizontal tailplane (Elevator section), and the engine gondolas.
  • The red “airberlin” titles on the forward fuselage and wing are decals / paint on white parts — not separate prints.

Defaults are a starting suggestion based on the photo, not gospel — adjust per part with the swatches. Elev Bolt and other hidden hardware are left white (colour irrelevant).
